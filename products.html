Products

We offer some great products and services for our wonderful customers and friends!

We offer basic and premium online service packages as well as a non-contract monthly deal.

Basic Service package

Features Included for members:

For the basic service package, a membership is required. Members pay $7.99/month for 24/7 online chat support and study aids to go with instructional videos.

Premium Service package

Features Included for members:

For the premium service package, a membership is required. Members pay $9.99/month for 24/7 online chat support, study aids to go with instructional videos and bonus features such as discounts to ticket events and extra video material.

Monthly Service package, no contract option

Features Included:

This package is for individuals who are unsure if they want to pay for a year's membership. There is a 30-day trial period, your card will not be charged. Subscribers pay $7.00/month. The first payment is due after the trial period ends. Benefits include the basic access, no online chat support. Subscribers can earn points to get access to study aids.
